Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 26, 2026

EchoToDo is built with a deep commitment to user privacy. We believe that your thoughts, notes, and productivity data belong to you and should remain completely private. This Privacy Policy details how the EchoToDo Chrome Extension handles data.

1. 100% Offline & Local Processing

Unlike traditional voice task applications, EchoToDo processes all speech and task generation entirely on your local machine.

  • No Cloud Storage: Your tasks, categories, and categories are saved directly in your browser's local sandbox storage (`chrome.storage.local`).
  • No External NLP APIs: EchoToDo employs a robust, client-side, rule-based natural language processor (NLP) to extract metadata (like priorities and tags) from your voice transcripts. No text is sent to third-party AI models or servers.
  • Zero Data Telemetry: We do not track your usage, clicks, domains visited, or count of tasks created.

2. Extension Permissions & Usage

To provide its core automated features, EchoToDo requests standard browser permissions. We only request the minimum permissions required for operation:

  • `storage`: Required to securely save your structured tasks locally in the browser so they persist between sessions.
  • `activeTab` & `tabs`: Used to detect the active website's domain name (e.g., `github.com` or `figma.com`) in order to automatically silo and display domain-specific checklists. The URL path, search queries, or tab content are never read or stored.
  • `audio` (Microphone access): Required to capture your voice memos via the native HTML5 Web Speech API for real-time local transcription.
  • `sidePanel`: Required to display your task checklist side-by-side with your active browser window for seamless productivity.
  • `notifications`: Used to trigger light user notifications when tasks are successfully added or completed.

3. Web Speech API Integration

Voice transcription is handled through the browser's native `SpeechRecognition` engine. Transcripts are converted to text inside your local browser runtime. At no point are your voice audio files or spoken words captured, saved, or uploaded to our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

EchoToDo operates without any backend infrastructure, third-party databases, tracking libraries, or external services. We do not share, sell, or distribute your local data to any third parties because we never have access to it in the first place.

5. Open Source Transparency

EchoToDo is open source and completely transparent. You can inspect our code, review the storage handlers, and verify our offline architecture directly in our public repository on GitHub.

6. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be reflected on this page. Because the extension has no network access capabilities, updates are distributed strictly through the official Chrome Web Store channel.

If you have any questions or feedback about our privacy practices, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
